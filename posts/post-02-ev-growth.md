**SLIDE 3 — WHAT IT MEANS**
- Label: `WHAT IT MEANS FOR DETAILERS`
- Headline: **EVS DON'T NEED LESS DETAILING. THEY NEED MORE.**
- Body: No engine bay — but the paint *is* the whole product now. EV buyers are protecting a $60k+ "tech investment" and treat presentation as status. Ceramic-coated cars resell for **~10–15% more** — exactly the number these owners care about.

**SLIDE 4 — CASE STUDY (TESLA)**
- Label: `WHAT THE DATA SHOWS · TESLA`
- Big stat: **30–50% THINNER**
- Body: Tesla's factory paint measures **30–50% thinner and softer** than BMW, Merc or Porsche (water-based eco paint: 3.5–5.0 mils vs 6–8). It chips and swirls fast. The result: Tesla owners spend **up to 300% more on paint repairs** — and the Model 3 & Y are now the two **most-PPF'd cars on the road.** *(Southern Tint Detail Pros; industry data, 2025)*
