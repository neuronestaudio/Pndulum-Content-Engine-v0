**SLIDE 4 — THE BIGGEST LEVER (CASE / SOURCE)**
- Label: `THE AUCTION MATH · GOOGLE`
- Headline: **RELEVANCE = LOWER COST**
- Body: Google charges you *less* when your ad matches the search. Lifting Quality Score from **5 to 8 cuts CPC 30–40%** — e.g. $5.50 → $4.00 drops CPL from **$79 to $57 with zero extra budget.** Relevance is the cheapest discount you'll ever get. *(Google ad auction; WordStream)*

**SLIDE 5 — HONEST COUNTERPOINT**
- Label: `THE HONEST COUNTERPOINT`
- Headline: **CHEAP LEADS CAN COST MORE.**
- Body: The lowest CPL can be a trap — rock-bottom leads on broad terms are often tyre-kickers who never book. Chase a low **cost per booked job**, not just a low cost per lead.
- Honest line: *Cheaper clicks only win if they still convert.*
