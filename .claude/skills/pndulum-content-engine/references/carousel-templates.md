# Carousel Templates

Three slide skeletons. Default length is **8 slides** (templates list the core
7 beats; add a dedicated big-name case-study slide). Rotate templates across
posts for variety.

## Template #1 — Diagnostic
For "you're solving the wrong problem" posts.
1. Big hook (contrarian cover)
2. The common assumption / instinct
3. The real diagnosis
4. Evidence / statistic
5. **Big-name case study** (what the giants know)
6. Honest counterpoint / business cost
7. Solution direction (DO IT MONDAY)
8. Brand position + CTA

## Template #2 — Revenue Leak
For "the money you're already leaving on the table" posts.
1. Hook (hidden/reveal cover)
2. Revenue leak #1
3. Revenue leak #2
4. **Big-name case study** (how a giant plugged it)
5. Financial cost (do the math)
6. Reality check / honest counterpoint
7. DO IT MONDAY
8. Brand position + CTA

## Template #3 — Industry Insight
For "the market is shifting, position now" posts.
1. Trend hook (split old→new cover)
2. The fact / statistic
3. What it means for detailers
4. **Big-name case study** (the data behind the trend)
5. The opportunity
6. The risk of inaction
7. The lesson (DO IT MONDAY)
8. Brand position + CTA

## Notes
- Every template carries the big-name case study + an honest counterpoint.
- The final slide always positions Pendulum and ends on one CTA.
- Match the cover device to the template (see brand-voice.md).
