## Australian / industry data (for "validated, local" posts)
- **IBISWorld (AU)** — 1,694 car wash & detailing businesses, 2025; market $613.7m; businesses −2.3%/yr; revenue sliding as customers trade down to cheap washes. (Near-census of the AU industry — strongest "validated" anchor.)
- **NAB SME Survey** — cash flow is the #1 concern for 43% of AU small businesses; ~80% took a hit.
- **AU SME data** — 64% of small businesses made lower profits YoY; tradies forced to compete on price.
- **QBE Sole Trader Survey** — customers now expect online quotes, instant replies, pro presence.
- **Missed-call / local stats** — 62% of home-service calls unanswered; 85% never call back; 62% call a competitor; 42% of enquiries after-hours (Aira; Ringeden; Numa).
- **SMB AI** — 58% of small businesses use AI (up from 23% in 2023); $3.70 ROI per $1; 2.3× more likely to grow (US Chamber; McKinsey; Salesforce).

## Sourcing rules (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
- **Never claim primary research Pndulum didn't do.** No "we surveyed 100 detailers"
  unless it actually happened and the findings are real. If you want that
  authority, anchor in a citable source (IBISWorld is a near-census — stronger
  than a small sample anyway). Fabricated stats destroy the exact trust the
  brand is built on.
- Prefer primary or well-known secondary sources; name them on-slide.
- One giant per post. Don't reuse the same giant on back-to-back posts unless the
  angle genuinely differs (Amazon-latency vs Amazon-conversion is OK).
- If a stat can't be verified live, drop it — never approximate.
